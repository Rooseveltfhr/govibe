About

An engineer's mind.
A builder's ambition.

Roosevelt Forestal is a computer engineer and entrepreneur who chose the hardest market in the hemisphere as his starting point — and turned it into a laboratory for world-class innovation. As Founder & CEO of GOVIBE Innovation Hub, he leads an ecosystem spanning artificial intelligence, software, education and entrepreneurship.

His conviction is simple: talent is universal — opportunity is not. GOVIBE exists to close that gap.

Vision & Mission

Not a company.
A movement.

Vision

A continental ecosystem

To build one of the largest innovation ecosystems born in Haiti — expanding across the Caribbean and into Africa, proving that world-class technology can rise from anywhere.

Mission

Talent meets opportunity

To equip a new generation with AI, engineering and entrepreneurship skills — and give them the infrastructure, capital pathways and networks to build companies that matter.

Method

Build, train, invest

Real products in the market. Real skills in the classroom. Real support for founders. GOVIBE operates at every layer of the innovation value chain — by design.
