Flagship

GOVIBE Innovation Hub

The nerve center of the ecosystem — where strategy, incubation, coworking and the innovation lab converge under one roof.

  • Problem — Haitian innovators work in isolation, without infrastructure, mentorship or networks.
  • Solution — A physical and digital home for builders: coworking space, innovation lab, incubation programs and a call center creating skilled jobs.
  • Impact — A growing community of founders, engineers and creatives shipping real products from Port-au-Prince.
  • Vision — Hubs replicated across Caribbean capitals, then African cities.

Education

GOVIBE Academy

A premium innovation academy with an international standard — training the region's next engineers, AI practitioners, and founders. Not theory for certificates: skills for careers.

  • Problem — Universities produce graduates; the market needs builders. The gap costs the region its best minds.
  • Solution — Intensive, practice-first programs in software engineering, AI, and digital skills, taught by practitioners who ship products.
  • Impact — Hundreds of professionals trained; alumni working across the ecosystem and beyond.
  • Vision — The reference academy for tech talent in the Caribbean, with online reach into Africa.

Technology

AI Solutions & Voice Agents

Applied artificial intelligence for real businesses — not demos. Automation, AI voice agents, intelligent customer service and data-driven decision tools, adapted to the realities of emerging markets.

  • Problem — Businesses in emerging markets are told AI "isn't for them yet." That's a myth — and a missed decade.
  • Solution — Practical AI deployments: voice agents that answer in the customer's language, workflow automation, AI-assisted operations.
  • Technology — Modern LLMs, speech interfaces, and integration engineering that works with local infrastructure.
  • Vision — Make Haitian businesses among the earliest AI-native adopters in the region, not the last.

Product Portfolio

SaaS & NFC Business Platforms

Flagship product: TAGTOA — an NFC & QR business platform giving every business in Haiti a complete digital presence: micro-sites, digital menus, payments, loyalty cards, events with NFC wallets, bookings and point-of-sale.

  • Problem — Most Caribbean businesses have no affordable path to digital tools built for their market.
  • Solution — One tap-and-go platform: NFC cards and QR codes connecting customers to menus, payments, loyalty and bookings — multilingual (FR·HT·EN·ES) and multi-currency.
  • Technology — Laravel SaaS architecture, NFC/Web-NFC, offline-capable PWA point-of-sale, double-entry wallet ledger.
  • Impact — Live in production, serving restaurants, events and service businesses.
  • Vision — The digital commerce layer for small business across the Caribbean.
